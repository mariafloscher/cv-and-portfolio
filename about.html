About me

Hi!! My name is Maria Fernanda Loscher. I was born in Caracas, Venezuela on October 2nd, 1993. All my life, I've loved art, drawing, creating, and most of all, being able to communicate visually. Having experience in different areas of visual communication, such as concept art, graphic design, illustration, motion graphics, portrait painting, character creation and, more recently, front-end developing, I'm constantly looking for new ways to not only communicate through visuals, but to challenge myself every day.
I'm passionate, driven, a great team player and curious about the world around me and the people in it.
This curiosity is one of the main reasons I became an artist. Our world and human beings inspire me everyday. I find inspiration even in the simplest things. I love to create stories, characters, creatures and places and turning them into reality with my graphic skillset.
With my art, I hope to some day inspire others to express themselves the same way as I've been inspired.
